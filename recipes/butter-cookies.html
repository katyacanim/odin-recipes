Butter Cookies

Description

Crisp, buttery, and melt-in-your-mouth delicious, butter cookies are a classic treat perfect for any occasion. Made with simple ingredients like butter, sugar, and flour, these cookies boast a rich, buttery flavor and a tender, crumbly texture.

Whether enjoyed plain, decorated with sugar, or dipped in chocolate, butter cookies are a timeless favorite that's easy to bake and even easier to love.

Ingridients

  • 200g soft butter
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 320g flour

Steps

  1. Mix well butter and sugar. Add egg yolks and mix. Add flower and mix.
  2. Wrap in clinfilm and put in the fridge for 1 hour.
  3. Take out, soften and shape into a sausage.
  4. Refrigerate for another 2 hours.
  5. Take out. Cut cookies, put on an oven tray lined with baking paper. Freeze for at least 15min.
  6. Oven at 170Â° for 25min.
  7. Enjoy.
