Lazy Dumplings

Description

Lazy dumplings are a quick and easy dish made from simple ingredients, perfect for a comforting meal. Typically, the dough is made with cottage cheese or ricotta, flour, eggs, and a pinch of salt.

Served with butter, sour cream, or a sprinkle of sugar, these soft, pillowy dumplings are a delicious and satisfying treat for any time of the day.

Ingridients

  • 500g cottage cheese
  • 4tbs sugar
  • 2 small eggs
  • 6-7tbs flour
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Blitz cheese and sugar. Add the rest of ingridients.
  2. Coat in flour and make small balls.
  3. Flatten a little with a fork and place in the freexer for 15-20 min.
  4. Bring to boil and cook for 2-3 mins.
  5. Serve with yogurt and fruits.
