Nectarine Galette

A nectarine galette is a rustic, free-form tart that's as beautiful as it is delicious. This simple yet elegant dessert highlights the natural flavors of the fruit, making it perfect for summer gatherings or a cozy treat any time of year.

Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for an irresistible finish.

Ingredients

  • 400g cottage cheese
  • 80g butter
  • 2tbs sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 150g flour
  • 1tbs baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 600-700g nectarines
  • 1-2tbs powdered sugar
  • 1tbs starch
  • milk
  • almond flakes

Steps

  1. Blend 200g of cheese, butter, 1tbs of sugar. Add egg yolks and blend.
  2. Add flour, baking powder and salt and mix.
  3. Refrigerate 15-20 mins.
  4. Slice nectarines and mix with cornstarch
  5. Roll dough on baking paper and lay half the nectarines.
  6. Blend 200g of cottage cheese with powdered sugar, spread over nectarines.
  7. Lay the rest of nectarines.
  8. Bend the edges of the dough inwards, brush with milk and sugar, and sprinkle some almond flakes.
  9. Bake at 170Â° for 30-35 mins.
  10. Enjoy.
